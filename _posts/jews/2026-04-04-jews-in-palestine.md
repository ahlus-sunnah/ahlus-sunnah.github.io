The gathering of the Jews in Palestine is among the signs of the Hour, because they gather in Palestine at the end of times awaiting the Dajjal (the Antichrist). The Dajjal will emerge among them, then Jesus, peace be upon him, will descend and kill him; their gathering in Palestine is thus one of the signs of the Hour. The Prophet, peace and blessings be upon him, informed us of this.
